About the Lodge

Hospitality, refined for the mountains.

Tucked into the Buspark area of Jumla, Nagarik Hotel & Lodge blends understated luxury with the soul of the Karnali. Every detail — from the bedding to the breakfast — is curated for travelers who want comfort without compromise.

  • Premium rooms with mountain views
  • Locally sourced, chef-prepared cuisine
  • Warm, attentive Nepali hospitality
  • Steps from Jumla Buspark — easy arrivals & trekking departures
